Entropy AI Club is a community of people who build with and learn about AI
together. We meet regularly, share what we're reading and building, and try
to lower the bar for anyone who wants to get more hands-on with AI.

This site is where that adds up over time. Every [post](/posts/), [resource](/resources/),
[tool](/tools/), [prompt](/prompts/), [project](/projects/) and set of
[meeting notes](/meeting-notes/) is attributed to the member who contributed
it, and shows up on that [member's own page](/members/) as part of their
timeline — so the club's history is also, individually, everyone's history.

## How the club works

- **Anyone can contribute.** Posts, resources, tools, prompts, projects and
  meeting notes all live as plain files in [our GitHub repository](https://github.com/entropy-ai-club/entropy-ai-club.github.io) —
  contribute via a pull request, or through the CMS at `/admin/` (which also
  opens a PR for review). Member roster changes are Git/PR only.
- **Everything is attributed.** Content is linked to the member(s) who made
  it, so contributions accumulate into a public track record.
- **The site is the record.** There's no separate internal wiki — what's
  published here *is* the club's shared memory.
